# Bug Reports — “Men’s Polo Shirt” Product Page

## BUG-001: The quantity of the item in the cart does not change after clicking the “Increase” button

# Environment
- Device: PC
- OS: Windows 10
- Browser: Opera
- Page: Cart page with the product “Men’s Polo Shirt”

# Preconditions
1. The cart page or tab is open.
2. The “Men’s Polo Shirt” item is in the cart.
3. The button to change the quantity is displayed in the cart.
4. The quantity is 1.

# Steps to reproduce
1. Click the button to increase the product quantity.
2. Check the product quantity in the cart.
3. Check the price and the total order amount.

# Actual result
The product quantity remains “1” after clicking the increase button.  
The price and the total order amount are not recalculated.

# Expected result
The quantity increases by 1 each time the “Increase Quantity” button is clicked.  
The price and total order amount are recalculated according to the quantity.

# Severity
Major

# Priority
High

Not attached

# Bug Reports — “Men’s Polo Shirt” Product Page

## BUG-002: The notification that the item has been added to the cart does not appear after clicking the “Add to Cart” button

# Environment:
Device: PC
OS: Windows 10
Browser: Opera
Page: “Men’s Polo Shirt” product page

# Preconditions:
1. The “Men’s Polo Shirt” product page is open.
2. The product is in stock.
3. An available color of the product is selected.
4. An available size of the product is selected.
5. The “Add to Cart” button is displayed on the page.

# Steps to reproduce:
1. Click the “Add to Cart” button.
2. Check whether a notification appears indicating that the product has been successfully added to the cart.
3. Open the cart and check whether the product has been added.

# Actual result:
A notification indicating that the product has been successfully added to the cart does not appear after clicking the “Add to Cart” button.
The product is added to the cart.

# Expected result:
After clicking the “Add to Cart” button, the user should see confirmation that the item has been successfully added to the cart, such as a notification, a change in the cart count, or the mini-cart opening.
The item should be added to the cart.

# Severity:
Minor

# Priority:
Medium

# Attachments:
None attached
